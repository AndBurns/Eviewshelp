@inv
Element Functions
Reciprocal function.
Syntax: @inv(x)
x: number, series
Return: number, series
Returns of the scalar , or of each value of in the series.
For scalars and series only. To obtain the reciprocals of the elements of a matrix object use @einv.
For matrix inversion, see @inverse.
Examples
show @inv(x)
returns a linked series whose elements are the reciprocal of those in the series x.
Cross-references
See also @einv, @emult, and @ediv.
