geomap
Geomap Declaration
Declare a geomap object.
Syntax
geomap geomap_name
Declare a geomap object that is capable of reading Esri (www.esri.com) shapefiles. Geomaps are useful for visualizing data that are tied to specific geographic regions.
Examples
geomap gmap
