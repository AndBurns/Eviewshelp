copy
Vector Procs
Creates a copy of the vector.
Creates either a named or unnamed copy of the vector.
Syntax
vector_name.copy
vector_name.copy dest_name
Examples
v1.copy
creates an unnamed copy of the vector V1.
v1.copy v2
creates V2, a copy of the vector V1.
