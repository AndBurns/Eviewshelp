copy
Sspace Procs
Creates a copy of the sspace.
Creates either a named or unnamed copy of the sspace.
Syntax
sspace_name.copy
sspace_name.copy dest_name
Examples
s1.copy
creates an unnamed copy of the sspace S1.
s1.copy s2
creates S2, a copy of the sspace S1.
