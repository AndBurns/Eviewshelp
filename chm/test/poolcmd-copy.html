copy
Pool Procs
Creates a copy of the pool.
Creates either a named or unnamed copy of the pool.
Syntax
pool_name.copy
pool_name.copy dest_name
Examples
p1.copy
creates an unnamed copy of the pool P1.
p1.copy p2
creates P2, a copy of the pool P1.
