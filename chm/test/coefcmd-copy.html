copy
Coef Procs
Creates a copy of the coef.
Creates either a named or unnamed copy of the coef.
Syntax
coef_name.copy
coef_name.copy dest_name
Examples
c1.copy
creates an unnamed copy of the coef C1.
c1.copy c2
creates C2, a copy of the coef C1.
