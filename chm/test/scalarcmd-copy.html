copy
Scalar Procs
Creates a copy of the scalar.
Creates either a named or unnamed copy of the scalar.
Syntax
scalar_name.copy
scalar_name.copy dest_name
Examples
s1.copy
creates an unnamed copy of the scalar S1.
s1.copy s2
creates S2, a copy of the scalar S1.
