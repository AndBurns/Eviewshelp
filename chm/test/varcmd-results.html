results
Var Views
Displays the results view of an estimated VAR.
Syntax
var_name.results(options)
Options
 
p
Print the view.
Examples
var mvar.ls 1 4 8 8 m1 gdp tb3 @ @trend(70.4)
mvar.results(p)
prints the estimation results from the estimated VAR.
