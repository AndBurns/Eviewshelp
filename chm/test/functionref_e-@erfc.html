@erfc
Element Functions
Complimentary (Gauss) error function.
Syntax: @erfc(x)
x: number
Return: number
.
for .
Examples
= @erfc(1/@sqrt(2))
returns 0.31731... (area under the normal curve beyond 1 standard deviation of the mean).
